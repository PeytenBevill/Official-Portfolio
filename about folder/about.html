About Me

Hello, my name is Peyten. I'm a recent graduate of Austin Coding Academy, where I have spent the past 9 months learning full stack web development. I was drawn to coding and web development because I was looking for ways to express my creativity. Being someone who isn't naturally gifted at drawing or your typical "creative outputs", it took me a while to figure out the best way to express myself. I have seriously enjoyed my time with Austin Coding Academy and I look forward to growing my skills even more in the work force.

I was born and raised in Austin Texas. I currently live in South Austin, but I grew up a little further south in a small town called Buda. Being in South Austin means I'm close to lots of hiking trails and outdoor activities. I love the Lady Bird Lake area. If I'm not on my paddle board or in a kayak on Lady Bird Lake, I'm probably hiking around it! I also frequent Mckinny Falls State Park with my dog for a nice hike.
